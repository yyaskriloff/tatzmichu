Our Mission

We believe that providing seminary students with funding to obtain the emotional health they need can jump start a happy life and ultimately a healthy Jewish home.

Tatzmichu is a non-profit organizationa that significantly subsidizes therapy costs for seminary students in Israel.We are dedicated to empowering young Jewish women, the future wives of Klal Yisrael, with the skills to cope with difficulties, improve resilience, process trauma and establish healty boundaries and habits so that each one can build a stable and healthy Bayis Neeman Bâ€™Yisrael.

Building Jewish Women

Building a Jewish Future

We live in a challenging world. Abuse, neglect, illness and trauma have created a need for significant mental health care, even within Torah communities. For many young Jewish women, the first time they spend a large period away from home is during the first year of seminary, making that year the most critical to receive mental health services. Unfortunately, many are unable to cover the cost. This leaves many young women vulnerable and at risk to become unhealthy adults and raise unhealthy families, thereby perpetuating a cycle of dysfunction and negativity.

Tazmichu is here to change that.

OUR IMPACT

By helping young Jewish women process and heal, we are enabling them to become their best selves who can build healthy and happy Jewish homes. We at Tatzmichu believe that the woman is the root of the home. Only healthy roots can blossom into vibrant and flourishing trees.
